Global Visa & Journeys is a London consultancy disrupting an industry built on shortcuts, false promises, and forged paperwork. We replace it with forensic preparation, radical transparency, and a 100% compliance record.

Our Story

Born from a Broken Industry.

The visa industry has long been split into two extremes. On one side, immigration lawyers charging £1,500 to £3,000 for advice that travellers don't quite need. On the other, an underworld of unregulated fixers — operators who promise the world, deliver fabricated documents, and leave clients exposed to ten-year bans.

The middle ground — where most genuine applicants actually live — was abandoned. So we built it.

Global Visa & Journeys was registered in England & Wales (Companies House No. 16959031) with one founding principle: that the visa application process deserves the same forensic rigour as a top London law firm, delivered with the warmth and accessibility of a trusted advisor. No fabricated documents. No false guarantees. No corners cut.

We saw families lose £2,000 in flight bookings to a single refusal letter. We saw students miss the start of term because a fixer had submitted a recycled cover letter copied from a Reddit thread. We saw professionals from Lagos, Banjul, Freetown and Accra carry the financial burden of repeat applications because nobody had told them the real reason their first attempt failed.

That ends with us. We sit in what the industry calls the Goldilocks Zone — the precision of legal advice without the price tag, and the logistics of a travel agent without the lack of expertise.

Our Mission

To make the bureaucracy invisible.

We exist to handle the strict legal logic, document forensics, and embassy compliance — so our clients can focus on the journey ahead.

Every application we touch must be capable of withstanding the most sceptical visa officer. Every narrative must be defensible. Every financial trail must be explainable. We disrupt the predatory visa industry by proving that ethical, transparent consulting is not only possible — it's the only model that works.

Our Values

01

Compliance First

No corners cut. No documents fabricated. No data manipulated. Clients who do not yet meet requirements are told upfront — and offered a 3 to 6 month Profile Rehabilitation plan, rather than paying for a likely refusal.

Legal Transparency

What We Are. What We Aren't.

Honest disclosure builds trust. Here's exactly where we sit, legally and professionally.

What We Are

  • A registered London consultancy: Companies House No. 16959031, England & Wales.
  • Covered by full Professional Indemnity Insurance (£500k limit).
  • UK GDPR compliant. Encrypted document storage. Strict client confidentiality.
  • Specialist in Schengen and EU visa strategy, document audit, and case construction.

What We Aren't

  • We are not a law firm and do not provide legal representation.
  • We are not OISC-registered — this is required only for UK immigration advice. We deal exclusively in Schengen and EU visas, which fall outside OISC scope.
  • We are not a Tour Operator and not ATOL-registered. We do not sell flight-inclusive packages.
  • We do not guarantee visa approval. Anyone who does is misleading you.
