Refusal Codes

Why Was Your Application Refused?

Understanding the real reason behind your refusal code is the first step to recovery

Code A3

Bank Statement Issues

Funds parking, income inconsistency, or a balance that drops to zero after the application period. The most common cause of West African refusals — and fully fixable with the right waiting strategy.

Code A6

Weak Ties to Home Country

The officer was not convinced you would return after your trip. No property, no dependants, no strong employment — nothing tying you to your home country.

Code A2

Unclear Purpose of Visit

Your travel itinerary was too vague or your stated reason for visiting did not match your financial profile or personal circumstances.

Code A1

Document Issues

The officer flagged a document as suspicious, incomplete, or inconsistent. This is the most serious code and requires careful handling before reapplication.

First-Time Traveller

No Travel History

First-time travellers face higher scrutiny. No previous stamps means no track record of returning home. This requires a strategic build before reapplying.

Previous Agent

Previous Agent Errors

Many clients come to us after another agent submitted an application that was poorly prepared. A refusal from a previous agent is recoverable — but must be handled differently from a fresh application.
